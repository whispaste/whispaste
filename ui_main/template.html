Smart Mode

Automatically refine every transcription with AI before it's pasted

When enabled, your transcription is sent to GPT-4o-mini for refinement before pasting

How it works
1 You record and transcribe as usual
2 The transcription is refined by AI using your chosen preset
3 The improved text is pasted — no extra steps needed

Choose a preset

Cleanup Fixes grammar, punctuation, and filler words — keeps your original meaning intact
Email Reformats your speech into a professional email with greeting and sign-off
Bullet Points Extracts key points from your speech and organizes them as a clear list
Formal Letter Rewrites your text in a professional, business-appropriate tone
AI Prompt Converts your speech into a clear, token-efficient AI instruction
Summary Condenses your speech into a concise summary with the key points
Notes Structures your dictation as organized notes with headings and action items
Translate Translates your transcription into the target language you choose below
